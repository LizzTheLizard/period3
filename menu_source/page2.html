Frieza is one of the villains in the Dragon Ball manga and the Dragon Ball Z anime. He also appears in several Dragon Ball Z movies, as well as Dragon Ball GT episodes. Frieza is a galactic emperor who runs the planet trade and is feared for his ruthlessness and power. He is the descendant of Chilled, the son of King Cold, the younger brother of Cooler, and the father of Kuriza.
